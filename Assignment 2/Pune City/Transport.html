Travel Information

Railways

Pune Railway Station: Major station connecting Pune to other cities across India. It’s well-connected with local transport options like buses and auto-rickshaws.

Lonavala and Khandala: Popular hill stations near Pune, easily accessible by train from Pune Railway Station.

Air Travel

Pune International Airport (PNQ): Located in Lohegaon, about 10 km from the city center. It handles both domestic and international flights.

Car Rentals

Car Rental Services: Numerous car rental companies are available, offering self-drive and chauffeur-driven options. It’s a convenient choice for exploring Pune and nearby areas.

Cycling and Walking

Cycling: Pune has several cycling routes and tracks. It’s a pleasant way to explore some of the city's scenic areas.

Walking: Many areas in Pune, especially around parks and markets, are pedestrian-friendly.
